Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

This website is a collection of simple games that runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Local Storage

Game scores and preferences may be stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by deleting your browser data.

No Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies. Your gameplay is completely private.

Third-Party Services

This website does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs that could access your data.

Contact

This is a personal project with no data collection. If you have questions about these games, you can review the open-source code.
